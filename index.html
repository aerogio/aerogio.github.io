CFD

As soon as I started learning about Computer Fluid Dynamics I could not stop my self. I had the possibilty to simulate point by point with fancy colors the dynamics of any flow. Even though it's not all puppy dogs and rainbows and the reality is much more complicated I was instantly blown away.

My Master thesis was the result of a mutual agreement between to Swedish companies: SCANIA and AFRY.

I spent six month of my life learning so many things. The importance of optimization, of strategic plans of being humble enough to ask for question.

GOAL: Minimizing Total Pressure drops.

Software: OpenFOAM.

Download the complete pdf-file of my thesis if you are interested.

BONSAI

One lazy sunny sunday afternoon I was strolling around with friends when we saw some little birds escaping from a flower shop. This old crazy Swedish lady was selling Rehab Bonsai with an interesting trunk S-shape for 50kr. Rehab Bonsai means that basically the tree was half dead, yours the challenge to revive it. I obviously won the challenge, which unfortunately made me fall into a bonsai black hole

GARY: My Rehab bonsai, which by the way went out from rehab pretty soon, is Gary. He is a Chinese Elm Bonsai, super cute and lately has also a son, Gary Junior growing from a cutting. I tried to cut several branches, but only one survived.

KAREN: My second in line is a small female rosemary bonsai, which in fact is not really a bonsai. Yet. From a normal rosemary bush I am trying to grow a bonsai, but she is having a hard time lately.

KIM: Kim is a beautiful example of non-binary japanese red maple bonsai, which my mum brought me for my birthday. Honestly I love it, but it should stay outside, and I don't really think it will survive the winter, but we will see.

EMACS upsets me everyday and my life has become emacs. I know that it is probably not great, yeah whatever.
